Come Over When You’re Sober, Pt. 2 is the second studio album by American artist Lil Peep, released posthumously on November 9, 2018. It serves as a follow-up to his debut album Come Over When You’re Sober, Pt. 1, and was completed after his death in November 2017. The project was brought to life by his close collaborators, especially producer Smokeasac and executive producer George Astasio of First Access Entertainment, with the support of Peep’s mother, Liza Womack.

The album showcases the signature sound that Lil Peep had become known for—a raw and emotional blend of emo, punk rock, and trap music. It explores themes of depression, loneliness, drug use, fame, and romantic frustration, all filtered through Peep’s distinctive melodic delivery and introspective lyrics. Compared to his earlier work.
