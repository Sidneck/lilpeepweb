Hellboy is a mixtape by American rapper and singer Lil Peep, released on September 25, 2016, via SoundCloud. Widely regarded as one of his most iconic and influential projects, Hellboy marked a turning point in Peep’s career—elevating him from an underground SoundCloud artist to a leading voice in the emerging emo rap movement. The mixtape combines gritty production, emotional lyricism, and a distinctive blend of genres, showcasing Peep’s unique ability to fuse punk, emo, and hip-hop into something entirely his own.

The project features production from key collaborators such as Smokeasac, Nedarb, Charlie Shuffler, and Horse Head, with beats that frequently sample early 2000s rock and emo bands. The title Hellboy, a reference to the comic book character, reflects Peep’s interest in dark, outsider imagery—something that runs throughout the mixtape’s tone and themes.
