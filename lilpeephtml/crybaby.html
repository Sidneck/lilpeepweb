Crybaby is a mixtape by American artist Lil Peep, released on June 10, 2016, through SoundCloud. It stands as one of his most beloved and influential projects, marking a significant moment in his evolution as a pioneer of the emo rap genre. Created during a particularly prolific and emotionally intense period in Peep�s life, Crybaby captures the raw essence of his early sound�melancholic, lo-fi, and deeply personal.

The mixtape features production from a range of underground producers, including Nedarb, Greaf, Smokeasac, and Lederrick.These beats often incorporate samples from 2000s emo, pop punk, and alternative rock bands, layered with trap percussion and hazy atmospheres. The result is a unique sonic blend that pairs emotional vulnerability with a gritty, DIY aesthetic.
