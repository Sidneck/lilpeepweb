Biography

Gustav Elijah Ã…hr (Swedish; November 1, 1996 November 15, 2017), known professionally as Lil Peep, was an American rapper and singer-songwriter. He was a member of the emo rap collective GothBoiClique. Helping pioneer an emo revival-style of rap and rock music, Lil Peep has been credited as a leading figure of emo rap music and served as an inspiration to outcasts and youth subcultures.

Despite his rising fame, Lil Peep struggled with personal demons, and on November 15, 2017, he tragically passed away at the age of 21 from an accidental fentanyl–Xanax overdose while on tour in Arizona.

Peep was also a fashion icon in underground circles, known for his tattoos, colorful hair, and unique personal style. He was part of the underground collective GOTHBOICLIQUE, which played a major role in shaping the SoundCloud rap scene.
